# Social card template

Use with `graphic-design-director` for share images, announcement cards, Xiaohongshu/Douyin covers, Twitter/X cards, and launch tiles.

## Formats

```text
square: 1080x1080
portrait social: 1242x1660 or 1080x1350
story/reel: 1080x1920
wide card: 1200x630
banner: 1500x500 or platform-specific
```

## Composition options

```text
big claim + proof chip
before/after split
single product screenshot + annotation
poster title + character/object
numbered carousel cover
```

## Copy hierarchy

```text
title: 5-14 Chinese characters or 3-7 English words
subtitle: one supporting line
proof chip: metric/source/date/category
CTA/tag: optional, small
```

## Rules

- Must read at thumbnail size.
- Use no more than two typefaces.
- Leave safe margins: at least 8% on portrait formats.
- One hook per card.
- Avoid full paragraphs and generic stock imagery.
- For Chinese social covers, use strong weight and high contrast.

## Open Design prompt add-on

```text
Create a social card with thumbnail-readable title, one visual hook, safe margins, platform-appropriate aspect ratio, and a clear proof chip. Avoid clutter and generic AI poster gloss.
```
