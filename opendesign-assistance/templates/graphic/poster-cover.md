# Poster and cover design template

Use with `graphic-design-director` for posters, covers, banners, thumbnails, and campaign visuals.

## Choose one composition

```text
hero-object: one object/person/device dominates
big-type: typography is the image
split-scene: two states collide
editorial-grid: magazine-like strict columns
sticker-board: layered ephemera, labels, crops
cinematic-frame: still from an imagined scene
```

## Required decisions

```text
format: 1:1 | 4:5 | 9:16 | 16:9 | 1200x630 | custom
platform: xiaohongshu | douyin | web hero | app store | social card
focal point: object/person/type/scene
copy hierarchy: title / subtitle / proof / CTA
brand temperature: clean | premium | chaotic | playful | industrial
```

## Quality gates

- Works as a small thumbnail.
- Title has enough contrast and breathing room.
- One visual idea, not a collage of unrelated effects.
- Background supports text; it does not fight it.
- Export-safe margins: 6-10% depending on platform.
- For Chinese covers, avoid overly thin fonts and cramped line breaks.

## Open Design prompt add-on

```text
Create a poster/cover with one strong composition system, clear typographic hierarchy, thumbnail readability, platform-safe margins, and a memorable visual hook. Avoid random gradients, stock-photo collage, and generic AI gloss.
```
