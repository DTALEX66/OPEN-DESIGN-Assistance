# Culture wall design template

Use with `graphic-design-director`, `uiux-layout-director`, or future spatial plugins for corporate culture walls, school/history walls,党建/荣誉墙, timeline corridors, lobby brand walls, and 中文文化墙 projects.

## Required brief

```text
site: lobby | corridor | reception | office | school | public hall
wall size: width x height, or approximate ratio
viewer distance: close reading | walking corridor | lobby glance
content type: mission | history | honors | values | timeline | people | product | public culture
brand tone: official | warm | tech | premium | youthful | cultural
production: print panel | acrylic | metal letters | lightbox | LED | mixed media
```

## Layout systems

Choose one dominant system:

```text
timeline ribbon: history/process/achievements
modular grid: values, honors, teams, products
hero statement: one large slogan + proof modules
museum strip: title, artifact, caption, sequence
layered relief: 2D graphics + raised type + lighting
interactive wall: QR/LED/touch/rotating content
```

## Design rules

- Design for viewing distance first; not every text block must be readable from far away.
- Use 3-level hierarchy: wall headline, section titles, close-reading captions.
- Keep real installation constraints: seams, panel widths, material joins, lighting glare.
- Avoid poster collage. A culture wall needs route, rhythm, and physical scale.
- Chinese official/culture projects need dignified spacing and stable type; avoid gimmicky gradients.

## Output contract

```text
1. Wall narrative
2. Zone map
3. Main composition system
4. Typography scale by viewing distance
5. Material/finish suggestions
6. Lighting/installation notes
7. production risk checklist
8. Open Design generation prompt
```

## Open Design prompt add-on

```text
Create a culture wall design with physical scale, viewing-distance hierarchy, material-aware panels, clear narrative zones, and installation realism. Avoid flat poster collage and unreadable decorative text.
```
