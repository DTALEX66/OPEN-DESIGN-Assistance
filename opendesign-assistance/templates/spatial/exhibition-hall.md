# Exhibition hall design template

Use for exhibition halls, 中文展厅, brand experience centers, museum-like rooms, showrooms, product demo spaces, and visitor centers.

## Required brief

```text
space type: exhibition hall | showroom | museum | brand center | product demo | government/public space
floor area: square meters or room ratio
visitor path: linear | loop | free exploration | guided tour
zones: intro | timeline | product | interactive | honor | future | rest/photo spot
media: print panels | LED wall | projection | model | AR/VR | touchscreen | physical artifacts
budget tier: economical | standard | premium | flagship
```

## Spatial sequence

```text
01 threshold: title, identity, first impression
02 orientation: map, thesis, visitor promise
03 narrative zones: ordered story modules
04 interaction points: touch, scan, watch, compare, operate
05 hero moment: photo-worthy central installation or LED wall
06 proof zone: data, honors, product cases, artifacts
07 future/CTA: next action, QR, contact, pledge, demo booking
```

## Design rules

- Plan path and sightlines before wall graphics.
- Each zone needs one sentence thesis and one visual anchor.
- Leave breathing areas; exhibition is not wall-to-wall information.
- Material palette should match brand tone and maintenance reality.
- Digital media must have fallback static state.
- Avoid overusing screens when physical models or lighting can create memory.

## Output contract

```text
1. Visitor journey
2. Floor/zone narrative
3. Key visual moments
4. Wall and media schedule
5. Material palette
6. 2D elevation prompt
7. 3D spatial render prompt
8. Construction/production risks
```

## Open Design prompt add-on

```text
Design an exhibition hall concept with visitor path, spatial zones, hero moment, wall elevation logic, media schedule, material palette, and realistic installation constraints. Avoid treating the hall as a flat poster board.
```
