# Pitch deck template

Use for investor decks, product strategy decks, internal proposals, and presentation artifacts.

## Six-slide bootstrap

```text
01 title: company/topic + sharp promise
02 problem: expensive pain with one concrete example
03 solution: product surface or workflow, not abstract diagram only
04 traction/proof: metric, user quote, prototype state, or market signal
05 model/plan: business model, roadmap, or go-to-market
06 ask/next step: decision required and why now
```

## Twelve-slide expansion

```text
01 title
02 one-line thesis
03 problem
04 current alternatives
05 solution
06 product demo flow
07 market/user segment
08 business model
09 traction/proof
10 competition/differentiation
11 roadmap
12 ask
```

## Visual rules

- One message per slide.
- Use visual evidence: product surface, chart, quote, diagram, screenshot.
- Keep slide titles as claims, not labels.
- Never make dense paragraphs on presentation slides.
- Reuse a strict grid and 2-3 accent colors.

## Open Design prompt add-on

```text
Create a concise pitch deck with claim-based slide titles, product-surface proof, one message per slide, and consistent visual system. Avoid dense consulting slides and generic startup fluff.
```
