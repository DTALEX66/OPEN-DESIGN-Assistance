# Visual design art-direction template

Use for visual design, key visuals, campaign systems, product visuals, editorial layouts, and style exploration.

## Required brief

```text
artifact: poster | website | app | exhibition | deck | social | packaging | event visual
message: the single idea viewers should remember
mood: calm | premium | energetic | official | playful | cinematic | industrial | cultural
medium: 2D graphic | 3D render | mixed media | photo-led | typography-led | illustration-led
constraints: brand colors, format, copy, production, platform
```

## Art direction axes

Define each axis before generating:

```text
composition: centered | asymmetric | grid | cinematic | collage | modular
visual density: sparse | medium | maximal
depth: flat | layered | isometric | 3D scene | photographic
type role: quiet label | graphic object | editorial headline | data label
color: monochrome | restrained | high contrast | cultural palette | neon/dark
texture: clean | paper | metal | glass | grain | holographic | industrial
```

## Output contract

```text
1. Visual thesis
2. Three art directions: safe / premium / memorable
3. Composition sketch in words
4. Type hierarchy
5. Color/material system
6. Image/render/illustration prompt
7. Production/export notes
8. QA checklist
```

## Open Design prompt add-on

```text
Create visual design art direction with one memorable visual thesis, explicit composition system, type hierarchy, color/material system, and three directions: safe, premium, memorable. Avoid random effects and generic AI gloss.
```
