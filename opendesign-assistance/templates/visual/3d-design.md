# 3D design and spatial render template

Use for 3D visual concepts, exhibition spaces, product renders, installation previews, isometric scenes, and environmental mockups.

## Required brief

```text
subject: product | booth | exhibition hall | culture wall | UI scene | installation | environment
view: front elevation | isometric | perspective | top-down | close-up detail
scale: object | room | hall | urban/public space
material: metal | acrylic | glass | fabric | wood | LED | stone | plastic | paper
lighting: softbox | museum | neon | daylight | dramatic | product studio
output: concept render | construction reference | mood render | product hero | spatial diagram
```

## 3D composition rules

- Establish camera height and focal length feeling.
- Use scale references: people, furniture, doors, screens, rails.
- Materials need roughness/reflectivity logic, not random shine.
- Lighting should support wayfinding and brand mood.
- For exhibition/culture wall, include seams, panels, floor/wall junctions.
- For UI/HUD 3D scenes, keep interface readability above visual effects.

## Output contract

```text
1. Scene intent
2. Camera/view direction
3. Spatial massing
4. Material palette
5. Lighting plan
6. Key objects and scale references
7. Render prompt
8. Production realism notes
```

## Open Design prompt add-on

```text
Create a 3D/spatial visual concept with camera direction, scale references, material palette, lighting plan, and production realism. Avoid impossible floating geometry and random glossy surfaces.
```
