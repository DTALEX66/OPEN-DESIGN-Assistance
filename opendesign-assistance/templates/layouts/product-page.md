# Product page layout template

Use for feature pages, app store-style pages, product detail pages, and product marketing screens.

## Structure

```text
01 product identity: name, category, one-line job
02 hero proof: product screenshot/device/mockup/demo state
03 core benefit: specific result, not generic quality claim
04 feature tour: 3-5 sections tied to screenshots or states
05 use cases: audience-specific cards
06 proof: metrics, testimonials, integrations, compatibility
07 specs/details: only after persuasion
08 CTA and next step
```

## Rules

- Product visual must show a real state, not blank chrome.
- Each feature section needs a before/after or user action.
- For apps, show navigation and primary workflow.
- For physical/digital hybrid products, show scale, context, and controls.
- Avoid marketing adjectives without interface proof.

## Open Design prompt add-on

```text
Design a product page centered on a realistic product surface, feature proof, use cases, and conversion. Avoid generic hero art and unsupported claims.
```
